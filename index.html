Scholar | Sanskritist | Researcher in Indian Knowledge Systems

About Me

I received my early education through the traditional Gurukul system, later pursuing specialized studies in Vyākaraṇa (Pāṇinian Grammar) under the classical Kashi tradition. I also underwent rigorous training in Nyāya Darśana (Indian Logic), Mīmāṁsā Darśana (Vedic Interpretation), Vedānta, and Śrauta Yajñas (Vedic Rituals)—both in theory and practice.
