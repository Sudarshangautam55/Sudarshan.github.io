About Me

Early education was completed through the traditional Gurukul system, followed by specialised studies in Vyākaraṇa (Paninian Grammar) under the classical Kashi tradition. Comprehensive training in Nyāya (Indian Logic), Mīmāṁsā (Vedic Interpretation), Vedānta, and Vedic Śrauta Yajñas (Rituals), both in theoretical and practical applications.
