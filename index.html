About Me

I am a dedicated scholar specializing in Sanskrit, Indian grammatical tradition, and humanistic studies. Currently pursuing PhD at IIT(BHU), Varanasi.

My research focuses on Pāṇinian grammar, Universal Semantic Representation, and the application of traditional knowledge to modern linguistics.
