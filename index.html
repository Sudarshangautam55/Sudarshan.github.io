I am a Ph.D. Scholar at IIT(BHU), Varanasi, deeply interested in Sanskrit, Nyaya, Vedanta, and Vedic rituals. I have trained in Gurukul tradition and actively participate in scholarly debates.

My research explores Pāṇinian grammar, the Ṣaṣṭhī case, and universal semantic representation, combining traditional and modern linguistic methodologies.
