About Me

Hello! I am Sudarshan Gautam, a researcher specializing in Linguistics, NLP, and multilingual computational frameworks. I began my education in a gurukula, a traditional residential system of learning in India, where I was immersed in classical knowledge under the guidance of my guru. This early education provided a deep understanding of discipline, values, and the holistic approach of traditional scholarship. I hold a BA and MA in Paninian Grammar and have undertaken extensive studies in major branches of Indian philosophy, including Nyāya Darśana, Mīmāṁsā Darśana, and other classical Shastras. I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computational Linguistics at IIT-BHU, Varanasi, focusing on Paninian Grammar.
